Get to know me!

I am a Sophomore in Electrical Engineering and extremely passionate about innovation, business and management, and international relations.

This past summer I interned at AARP's Tech Nest in Research Park where I ideated from scratch and worked on two projects, one with the Google Home to make a skill which helps solve the problem of social isolation amongst the elderly, and the other on making a bot for Coratana and Skype to automate certain business functions for AARP, a project which has seen a really positive response from the organisation, because of which I will be working on the same automation project this coming Fall Semester.
I am the Founding President of the Illini Internet of Things Association, the first IoT focused student organisation at UIUC. In this role I have been able to grow the organisation at a rapid pace with membership numbers continuously rising as well growing partnerships with the industry.
I am also a consultant for OTCR Consulting the premier student run consulting firm on campus
